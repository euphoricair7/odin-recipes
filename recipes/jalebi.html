Jalebi!

Jalebi,the ultimate comfort food. There's nothing more comforting than the warm, syrupy goodness of jalebis. It is a spiral of happiness. It has such a sweet and tangy sensation, it is a dessert that brings back memories of festivals and celebrations.

Ingredients:

    3 cup all purpose flour
    2 cup hung curd
    1/2 cup ghee
    3 cup sugar
    5 strand saffron
    1/2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom
    1/2 cup corn flour
    1 1/2 pinch baking soda
    2 cup sunflower oil
    3 cup water
    4 drops rose essence
    1/2 teaspoon edible food color

Steps:

    Step 1 Prepare the Jalebi batter and let it ferment overnight
    Step 2 Deep fry the Jalebis
    Step 3 Soak the Jalebis in sugar syrup and serve
